ASTN

The mission of the Australasian Sleep Trials Network is to conduct and facilitate clinical trials of national and international significance that will have a major impact on sleep health. It will achieve this mission principally through investigator driven trials, funded by government or industry.

Sleep Health Questionnaire

Sleep Health Questionnaire is a standardised battery of sleep related questionnaires.

AusEd Driving Simulator

AusEd driving simulator - assessment of fatigue

The AusEd driving simulator is sensitive to performance decrement from fatigue in the laboratory setting, potentially making it useful as a laboratory or office based test for fatigue risk management.
